## First-Pass Prompt

Painterly historical realism for a children's Revolutionary War picture book. Scene focus: At last the men grew so desperate they roasted their shoes and ate them. Even that was not enough. Illustration direction: stark campfire scene with a boot or shoe near flame, soldiers gaunt and weary, handled with restraint rather than shock. Make this an object-led composition, not a character portrait. The shoe and low fire are the focal point, positioned low in frame, with weary soldiers pushed back in softer shadow. Intimate but restrained camera, near still-life feeling, small warm core inside cold darkness. Avoid any large foreground face. Keep upper area simpler for text.
