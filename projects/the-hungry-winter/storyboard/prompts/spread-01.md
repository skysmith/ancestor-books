## Story Text

The ground was cold.

## Illustration Note

## First-Pass Prompt

Painterly historical realism for a children's Revolutionary War picture book. Scene focus: Winter was hard on the soldiers. The wind cut through their coats. The ground was cold. The days were long. And the food was almost gone. Illustration direction: snowy or barren winter camp, soldiers hunched against wind, muted palette, immediate feeling of cold. Use a true wide establishing composition, not a portrait. Daniel should be small within the environment, one figure among several, with the camp and weather as the main subject. High horizon, large open cold sky, broad gray-blue field, very little firelight, no close face dominating the frame. Text-safe negative space in the sky. Textured brushwork, subtle period costume detail, no graphic violence.
