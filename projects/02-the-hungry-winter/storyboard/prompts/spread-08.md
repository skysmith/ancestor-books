## First-Pass Prompt

Painterly historical realism for a children's Revolutionary War picture book. Scene focus: Daniel was chosen to stand guard. The others moved ahead through the night. The air was sharp and still. Illustration direction: split focus between Daniel holding watch and shadowy figures moving toward a distant Tory property. Use a split-emphasis composition: Daniel placed to one side in still profile or three-quarter silhouette, while several smaller figures recede into the distance on the opposite side toward a faint property light. This should feel spatial, not portrait-like. Keep the night brighter and more legible with moonlight, snow bounce, or distant lantern glow. Daniel separated rather than centered. Protect upper sky for text.
