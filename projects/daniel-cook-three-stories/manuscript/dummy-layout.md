# Daniel Cook: Three Stories from the Revolution

## 24-Page Dummy Layout

Format assumption: 24 interior pages. The cover is separate. Page 1 is a right-hand title page; page 24 is the final left-hand page before the back cover.

The book works best as one continuous arc: Daniel survives gunfire, hunger, deception under pressure, and judgment. The repeated thread is not battle glory. It is endurance.

## Flow Check

This 24-page structure is tight but workable. It keeps the book from becoming three disconnected episodes by using hunger and survival as the bridge:

- The gunfire story proves Daniel can survive fear.
- The honey story shows hunger turning into risk and quick thinking.
- The pork story raises the moral stakes: Daniel breaks an order, but does it to feed the men.

The pork story needs the most new art because the archived Daniel work mostly covered bullets, honey, winter hunger, and the dog.
