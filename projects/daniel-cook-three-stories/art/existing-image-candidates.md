The pork story is not directly covered by the old selected-image set. Plan new art for:

- Page 22: Daniel leaving camp at night after the captain says he cannot spare him.
- Page 23: Daniel returning after eight or ten days with pork on his back.
- Page 23: Daniel under court-martial, standing before officers.
- Page 24: Daniel dividing the pork and the soldiers sharing a quiet feast.
