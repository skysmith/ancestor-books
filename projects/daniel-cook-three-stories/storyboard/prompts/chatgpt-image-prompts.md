Overall style: painterly historical realism for a children's picture book, matching the reference image's blue-gray winter atmosphere, soft brush texture, restrained firelight, and emotionally serious tone. No text, no letters, no numbers, no watermark, no speech bubbles, no logos, no modern clothing, no modern objects. Keep violence non-graphic and child-appropriate.
