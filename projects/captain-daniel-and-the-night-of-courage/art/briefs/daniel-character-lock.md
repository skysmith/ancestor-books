## Identity Core (copy exactly into every Daniel prompt)

Young colonial soldier, approximately 16–18 years old, slim build, narrow jaw, soft youthful cheeks, anxious but steady eyes, medium-small straight nose, natural lips, short brown hair mostly hidden under a worn tricorn hat, clean-shaven, emotionally readable face, vulnerable but brave.

## Costume Core

- Worn tricorn hat (consistent shape and scale)
- Off-white/gray linen-wool shirt
- Brown leather shoulder strap
- Period-correct colonial soldier styling
- No modern fasteners or fabrics

## Expression Set (approved)

1. Quiet alertness
2. Fear under control
3. Shock/freeze beat
4. Determined effort
5. Reflective fatigue

## Drift Prevention (negative block)

Do not age Daniel into a mature adult. Avoid: square heroic jaw, stubble/beard, bodybuilder proportions, glamorized action-hero face, anime eye exaggeration, face redesign, inconsistent nose/eye spacing, different hat shape.

## Allowed Variation

- Lighting can vary by scene (moonlight, firelight, dawn)
- Dirt/sweat/smoke can vary by action intensity
- Expression changes are allowed, facial structure is not

## Prompt Construction Rule

Every Daniel image prompt must include:
1. Identity Core (verbatim)
2. Costume Core
3. Scene-specific action/composition
4. Drift Prevention block

## QC Checklist Per Image

- Reads as same young person as reference-b
- Hat shape consistent
- Eye/nose proportions consistent
- No adult-aging drift
- Emotional readability preserved in low light

If any fail: regenerate before using in spread comps.
