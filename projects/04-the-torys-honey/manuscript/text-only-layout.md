### Spread 13 (Pages 29-30)

The next morning
the old Tory came to the army camp

### Spread 14 (Pages 31-32)

The captain opened the chest.
Honey gleamed on a soldier's hand.
The old Tory watched.
Daniel answered quickly.
The captain said,
"He has the toothache."

Then the old man left.

And the soldiers looked at one another.

And laughed.
