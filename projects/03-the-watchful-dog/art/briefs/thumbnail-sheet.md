# Thumbnail Sheet

## Purpose

Rough composition guide for the full dummy. This sheet solves suspense, staging, and text-safe space before any detailed art pass.

## Global Composition Rules

- Keep the night readable through silhouette and light control.
- Use distance and darkness to imply the larger plan while keeping Daniel central.
- Let the dog and doorway scenes carry the strongest contrast.
- End with reveal and quiet payoff, not a heroic finish.

## Value Strategy

- Opening: cold winter camp
- Middle: sharp darks, doorway light, musket flash
- Ending: honey glow as subtle reward in darkness
