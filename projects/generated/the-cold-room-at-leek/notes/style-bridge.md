# Style Bridge

`The Cold Room at Leek` should reuse the approved Sieger anchor and the same drawing language established for `Sieger Rides through the Snow`.

Keep:

- Sieger as a slim Dutch young man around `25`
- pen line and colored pencil on warm white paper
- detail concentrated near Sieger and the human moment that matters
- edges falling away into white paper
- restrained Dutch village imagery when natural
- cool gray-blue winter palette with small amber warmth

Shift slightly for this story:

- less travel grandeur
- more interior plainness
- more tactile surfaces: sackcloth, wood, roofline, blankets
- compassion expressed through practical action rather than journey scale
