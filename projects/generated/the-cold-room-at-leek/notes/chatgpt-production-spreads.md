## Global Character Rules

- Sieger must match the approved anchor image
- keep him slender, earnest, and practical
- avoid making him look like a child
- avoid over-rendered facial detail
- avoid glossy cinematic realism
- avoid mechanically precise objects if a looser illustrated treatment feels truer

## Quick Revision Targets

If the first generation round drifts, steer back toward:

- more white paper and less filled background
- less face detail
- more tactile cloth, wood, and roof surfaces
- same Sieger hair and face from the approved anchor
- Dutch imagery that feels natural, not decorative for its own sake
