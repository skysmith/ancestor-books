## Character Read

From now on, Sieger should read as:

- about `25`
- slim and lanky
- gentle, earnest, and slightly formal
- subtly masculine rather than boyish
- longer face
- a little more structure in brow, nose, and jaw
- still soft enough for a picture book

Avoid:

- child proportions
- round toddler-like face
- fashion-pretty glamor
- rugged heroic-man treatment
- heavy adult bulk

## Style Read

Use this image to keep:

- pen-and-wash / colored-pencil feeling
- warm off-white paper
- whispery edges
- airy snow atmosphere
- soft vintage illustration charm
- emotionally quiet storytelling

## Spread Format

All new book images should now target:

- true `2:1` horizontal picture-book spreads
- wide composition with breathing room for text if needed
- atmospheric Dutch environments when they arise naturally from the scene

Dutch visual cues are welcome when they feel organic:

- distant windmills
- low Dutch roads
- canals or ditches
- church spires
- flat winter horizon
- sparse farm buildings

Do not force Dutch motifs into every image.
