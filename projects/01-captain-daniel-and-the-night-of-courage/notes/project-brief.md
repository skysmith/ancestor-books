# Project Brief

## Core Idea

Turn an ancestor story about Daniel Cook into a cinematic, emotionally clear children's picture book.

## Tone

- Tense but not gruesome
- Courage under pressure
- Reflective ending rather than triumphant battle ending

## Key Visual Motifs

- Stone wall
- Moon and shadow
- Musket flash
- Hat with bullet hole

## Editorial Questions

- Should "Friend to the States" remain as-is for historical voice, or be simplified for younger readers?
- Is "I give up!" the strongest line, or should the wording be tuned for read-aloud clarity?
- Should page 32 remain story text, or should an author's note/end matter follow the final spread in a future expanded edition?
