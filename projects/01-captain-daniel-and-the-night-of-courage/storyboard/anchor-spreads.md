# Anchor Spreads

## Goal

These six spreads define the book's look, pacing, and emotional arc. If they work, the rest of the book becomes much easier to finish consistently.

## Spread 1

- Establish the road, the walls, and Daniel's scale
- Mood: quiet dread
- Must-have: Daniel small in frame, walls tall, moonlight restrained

## Spread 2

- First reveal of danger
- Mood: shock
- Must-have: silhouettes rising behind the wall, outnumbered feeling

## Spread 4

- Explosive turning point
- Mood: flash and chaos
- Must-have: musket flash dominating the composition

## Spread 7

- Physical peak of the story
- Mood: desperate survival
- Must-have: Daniel young, frightened, climbing a truly difficult wall

## Spread 10

- Emotional comedown
- Mood: quiet aftermath
- Must-have: intimate firelight, subtle injury, inward expression

## Spread 14

- Final emotional landing
- Mood: distilled, still, reflective
- Must-have: simple composition with space for silence
