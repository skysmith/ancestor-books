Description:
Drawn from Sieger Springer's 1900 journal, this illustrated picture book gathers two true winter journeys from Holland. In the first story, Sieger rides through wind and snow to Arnhem to help after a death in the Saints' community. In the second, he visits a family in Leek and finds one small practical way to make a sick woman's room warmer. Together these stories remember faith, endurance, and kindness in the cold.
